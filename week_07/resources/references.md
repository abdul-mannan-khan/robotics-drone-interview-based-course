## Key Concepts
- **MAVLink**: Lightweight messaging protocol for drone communication
- **MAVROS**: ROS/ROS2 node that bridges MAVLink and ROS topics/services
- **Offboard Mode**: External computer controls the drone via setpoints
- **SITL**: Software-In-The-Loop simulation for testing without hardware
- **Failsafe**: Automatic safety responses to failure conditions
