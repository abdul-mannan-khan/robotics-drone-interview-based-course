# Week 11 References: Control Systems - PID to MPC

## Textbooks

1. Ogata, K. "Modern Control Engineering" (5th Edition), Prentice Hall, 2010.
2. Astrom, K.J. and Murray, R.M. "Feedback Systems: An Introduction for Scientists and Engineers", Princeton University Press, 2021.
3. Borrelli, F., Bemporad, A., and Morari, M. "Predictive Control for Linear and Hybrid Systems", Cambridge University Press, 2017.
4. Beard, R.W. and McLain, T.W. "Small Unmanned Aircraft", Princeton University Press, 2012.

## Key Papers

- Mellinger, D. and Kumar, V. "Minimum snap trajectory generation and control for quadrotors", ICRA 2011.
- Kamel, M. et al. "Model Predictive Control for Trajectory Tracking of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles Using ROS", Springer, 2017.
- Falanga, D. et al. "The Foldable Drone: A Morphing Quadrotor that can Squeeze and Fly", IEEE RA-L, 2019.

## Software

- scipy.linalg.solve_continuous_are - Riccati equation solver
- scipy.optimize.minimize (SLSQP) - Constrained optimization for MPC
- ros2_control - ROS2 control framework
- OSQP - Operator Splitting QP solver
- ACADOS - Fast nonlinear MPC solver
