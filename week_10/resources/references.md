# Week 10: Computer Vision for Drones - References

## Textbooks
- Hartley & Zisserman, "Multiple View Geometry in Computer Vision", 2nd Ed.
- Szeliski, "Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications", 2nd Ed. (2022)
- Ma, Soatto, Kosecka, Sastry, "An Invitation to 3-D Vision"

## Camera Models & Calibration
- Zhang, "A Flexible New Technique for Camera Calibration", IEEE TPAMI, 2000
- DLT (Direct Linear Transform): Hartley & Zisserman Ch. 7

## Feature Detection
- Harris & Stephens, "A Combined Corner and Edge Detector", 1988
- Lowe, "Distinctive Image Features from Scale-Invariant Keypoints", IJCV 2004

## Homography & RANSAC
- Fischler & Bolles, "Random Sample Consensus", Comm. ACM, 1981

## Object Detection
- Dalal & Triggs, "Histograms of Oriented Gradients for Human Detection", CVPR 2005

## Stereo Vision
- Scharstein & Szeliski, "A Taxonomy and Evaluation of Dense Two-Frame Stereo", IJCV 2002

## Optical Flow
- Lucas & Kanade, "An Iterative Image Registration Technique", 1981
- Horn & Schunck, "Determining Optical Flow", Artificial Intelligence, 1981

## Drone Vision
- Scaramuzza et al., "Visual Odometry", IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine, 2011
- Forster et al., "SVO: Semi-Direct Visual Odometry", ICRA 2014
