# Week 2 References: Sensor Fusion with Extended Kalman Filter

## Textbooks

1. **Probabilistic Robotics** — S. Thrun, W. Burgard, D. Fox (MIT Press, 2005)
   - Chapters 3–4: Gaussian Filters (KF, EKF, UKF)
   - The definitive reference for state estimation in robotics

2. **State Estimation for Robotics** — T. D. Barfoot (Cambridge, 2017)
   - Rigorous mathematical treatment of Kalman filtering and beyond

3. **Kalman and Bayesian Filters in Python** — R. Labbe (Free online)
   - https://github.com/rlabbe/Kalman-and-Bayesian-Filters-in-Python
   - Interactive Jupyter notebooks, excellent for learning

## Papers

- R. E. Kalman, "A New Approach to Linear Filtering and Prediction Problems," *Journal of Basic Engineering*, vol. 82, pp. 35–45, 1960.
- G. Welch and G. Bishop, "An Introduction to the Kalman Filter," University of North Carolina, TR 95-041, 2006.
- S. J. Julier and J. K. Uhlmann, "Unscented filtering and nonlinear estimation," *Proceedings of the IEEE*, vol. 92, no. 3, pp. 401–422, 2004.
